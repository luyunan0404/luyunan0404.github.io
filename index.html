Yunan (Lucy) Lu

Ph.D. Student, Computer Science · Columbia University

I'm a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Columbia University advised by Zhou Yu. My research focuses on the evaluation of application-centric LLM-based agent systems, particularly through simulation-driven methods for assessing agent behavior, modeling realistic user interactions, and improving system performance via continuous learning.

I'm happy to chat about research or collaborations — reach me at yl4021@columbia.edu.
